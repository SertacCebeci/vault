# Harry and Rosemary Wong

Authors of the bestselling *The First Days of School*, a practical guide for teachers on establishing classroom structure from the very first day. Their approach emphasizes anticipating students' first-day questions and addressing each one proactively to ease the transition from unstructured time to the school environment.

## Key contributions
- Developed a systematic first-day checklist approach: anticipate students' questions (Am I in the right room? Where do I sit? Who is my teacher? What will we be doing? What are the expectations?) and plan answers for each → [[classroom-procedures]]
- Emphasized that the first days of school set the procedural foundation for the entire year → [[procedural-learning-system]]
