# Barak Rosenshine

Barak Rosenshine was an educational psychologist who synthesized decades of research on effective teaching into a set of principles of effective instruction. His framework provides a practical checklist for [[direct-instruction]]: begin with review of prior learning, present new material in small steps with student practice after each, limit information load, give clear explanations, ask many questions, provide guided practice with feedback, use think-alouds and worked examples, and prepare students for independent practice.
