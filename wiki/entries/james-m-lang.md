# James M. Lang

Professor of English and director of the D'Amour Center for Teaching Excellence at Assumption University in Worcester, MA. Author of *Small Teaching* (2016, 2nd ed. 2021), *Small Teaching Online* (with Flower Darby, 2019), *Distracted* (2020), and *Cheating Lessons* (2013). Writes a monthly column on teaching for the *Chronicle of Higher Education*. His central contribution is the "small teaching" framework: small, evidence-based modifications to course design and classroom practice that can be implemented immediately by busy faculty.
