# Doug Lemov

Author of *Teach Like a Champion 2.0*, a widely influential book on classroom teaching techniques. Lemov's work is grounded in direct observation of high-performing teachers and distillation of their practices into replicable techniques.

## Key contributions
- Articulated the paradox of classroom culture: trying to ignore behavior leads to talking about nothing else, while being intentional about behavioral culture allows it to "fade into the background" → [[classroom-procedures]]
- Emphasized that effective classroom culture is nearly invisible because it is proactively built, not reactively managed → [[procedural-learning-system]]
- Recommends using atypical time increments for group work (e.g., 4 minutes instead of 5) so students treat the deadline as real → [[collaborative-learning-procedures]]
