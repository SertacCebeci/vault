# Barbara Oakley

Engineering professor and co-instructor of the "Learning How to Learn" Coursera MOOC — one of the world's most popular online courses. Her personal story embodies the course's central message: she was self-described as the "belligerent queen of anti-math," hating and failing at math and science through grade school, middle school, and high school. She enlisted in the army, learned Russian, worked as a translator on Soviet trawlers in the Bering Sea, and as a radio operator at the South Pole Station in Antarctica. She only began studying math and science at age 26, eventually becoming a Professor of Engineering — demonstrating that learning ability is not fixed.

- Co-created "Learning How to Learn," making neuroscience-based learning strategies accessible to millions → [[focused-and-diffuse-modes]], [[chunking]], [[procrastination]]
