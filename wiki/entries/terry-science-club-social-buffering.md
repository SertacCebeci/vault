# Terry's High School Science Club

**Concepts illustrated:** [[social-buffering]], [[collaborative-learning]]

Coauthor Terry Sejnowski had been branded a troublemaker in high school for his many questions. A high school club for science aficionados saved him — it gave him direction, a way to bond with kindred spirits, and taught him the beginnings of leadership skills that he uses to this day. The group provided what he could not find in the regular classroom: peers who shared his intensity and curiosity.

## Why it matters
Terry's story illustrates social buffering at its most transformative. A student labeled as disruptive in one context found a supportive group in another, and that group moderated the social stress of not fitting in while channeling his energy productively. It is a reminder that the right collaborative environment does not just improve academic outcomes — it can redirect a student's entire trajectory.
