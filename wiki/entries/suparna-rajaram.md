# Suparna Rajaram

Professor of psychology at Stony Brook University whose research examines how group dynamics affect cognitive output. Her work demonstrates that people in larger groups generate fewer ideas than when working in smaller groups or alone, and become less receptive to outside ideas — a finding with direct implications for how teachers structure collaborative learning.

## Key contributions
- Research on group size and idea generation → [[collaborative-learning]]
- Evidence supporting [[brainwriting]] as a hybrid individual-then-group approach
