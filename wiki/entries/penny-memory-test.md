# The Penny Memory Test

**Concept illustrated:** [[illusions-of-competence]]

You've seen a penny thousands of times, yet when presented with a dozen different images of pennies (only one correct), most people can't identify the right one. Mere exposure — even repeated over a lifetime — does not create durable, retrievable memory.

## Why it matters

Demolishes the intuition that repetitive exposure equals learning. Seeing something over and over is not the same as knowing it.
