# The UCLA Fire Extinguisher

**Concept illustrated:** [[illusions-of-competence]]

Faculty and students at UCLA's Psychology Building were asked to locate the fire extinguisher closest to their office. Most failed. One professor who had worked there for 25 years went looking and discovered it was right next to his office door — inches from the doorknob he turned every day. Years of repetitive exposure had produced zero retrievable knowledge.

## Why it matters

Even in a building full of psychologists, passive exposure doesn't build memory. Active engagement is required.
