# Lens — Illustration

## Question

Could you tell this entry as a story — does it have a protagonist, a setting, and an outcome?

## How to apply

An illustration brings a concept to life with a specific, concrete example. Two subtypes both match:

- **Empirical study** — a controlled experiment with subjects, conditions, and measurable outcome.
- **Narrative exemplar** — a real-world anecdote or parable illustrating a principle.

If the candidate can be retold as "eight-year-olds tossed beanbags in gym class and the varied-distance group outperformed the fixed-distance group" — match. If the candidate is "varied practice builds flexible schemas" — no match; that's the concept the illustration exemplifies.

Slugs name the **event**, not the **idea**: `beanbag-toss-study`, not `varied-practice-evidence`.
