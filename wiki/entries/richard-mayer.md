# Richard Mayer

Educational psychologist who has spent decades studying best practices for teaching in multimedia formats. His research established the foundational principles of [[multimedia-learning-theory]], demonstrating that combining visual and verbal information leverages the dual-channel architecture of [[working-memory]] to improve learning outcomes. Many of his findings apply to both online and face-to-face instruction.

## Key contributions
- [[multimedia-learning-theory]] — established the empirical basis for dual-channel instructional design
